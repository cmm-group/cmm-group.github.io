RSC Solid State Chemistry Group Christmas Meeting 2018

Hosted by the University College London, the 38th annual Christmas Meeting took place on the 17th December for two days. Members of the Group that attended were selected to present posters.

UK's HEC Materials Chemistry Consortium

Hosted by the University of Lincoln, entitled "Modelling of Advanced Functional Materials using Terascale Computing" the event showcased the work produced using national HEC resources. The event was hosted on the 3rd of Septmember 2018 for three days. Members of the group attending presented posters highlighting their research performed on the national HEC resource ARCHER.
